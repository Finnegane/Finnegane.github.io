  JONATHAN WILLIAMS

Jonathan Williams

Recreation Program Specialist
for the City of San Marcos
Jonathan Williams is a Recreation Program Specialist who works for the City of San Marcos to enrich the lives of the members in the community.

Jonathan believes the key to enriching people’s lives is through the classes, activities, and special events the city provides for the community.

Jonathan has worked for the City of San Marcos Since July 2015 as a Front Desk Attendant, but was soon promoted to coordinate transportation services. He was finally promoted in September 2016 to oversee classes, activities, and special events.

Jonathan is proficient in: Microsoft Office Suite, Adobe Creative Suite, ActiveNet (facility management, online class registration, point of sales), and Web Development.

Jonathan holds an Associate of Arts Degree in Math & Science from Palomar College.
